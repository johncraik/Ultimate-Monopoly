## 2. 🌟 Dynamic card display (`CardDisplayHelper`) — reviewed
John made card/group text **dynamic**: a `{G<group>__<action>}` tag in the card text (bare
`{<action>}` in a group's text) is replaced at display time with the `MoneyAction` amount, after
rounding (and the %cap for a percentage action). `GroupKey` ("G0"/"G1") drives the replacement.
- 🔴 **Caught a real defect:** `FormatCardText` applied the %cap to **every** non-multiplied
  money action, but `RealiseAmount` only caps when `PercentageApplies`. So a plain `{G0__0}`
  charge displayed cap-scaled (£300 → £30 at 10% cap) while resolution paid full. **John fixed**
  — gated the cap on `moneyAction.PercentageApplies` (rounding stays unconditional).
- Wired `CardService` to render through `GetDisplayText` (draw acknowledge + `CardOptionPrompt`
  body + per-option group labels) so players never see raw `{G0__0}` tags.

## 5. 🌟 `IncludeHolderInRoll` (John's steer)
Highest/lowest-roller cards "often (not always) let the drawer roll too". Added
`MoneyAction.IncludeHolderInRoll` (default false). When true, the holder joins the dice-off
(`GetPlayers(holder, excludePovPlayer: !includeHolder)` — John's tidier ternary); when the holder
**wins their own dice-off**, the movement would be with themselves → **no-op** (return before any
transaction). So "pay the highest roller, you roll too — roll high to keep your money" works
literally. Tie-break keeps the first clockwise roller (holder first → wins ties → flagged as a
slight drawer advantage).

## 10. 🌟 Card triggers — designed (`card-triggers.md`)
The big architectural piece: a **`CardTriggerService`** called at every trigger point that
evaluates held cards and drives prompt/force/apply/suppress. Worked through it with John; wrote
the full doc (18 sections). Decisions (John's calls):
- **Turn-scope = is the holder the *subject*** (CardholderTurn) **vs a bystander reacting**
  (AnyPlayerTurn) → scan the subject's hand vs everyone's. Reconciles advance-5-on-third-die
  (the holder is the subject being moved). The `cards-actions.md` `Self/Other` tags need
  re-reading on this basis.
- **Context-aware amount** = a `MoneyAction.AmountSource { Fixed, TriggerAmount }` (the modifier
  family — same precedent as `PercentageApplies`/`DiceMultiplier`/`PerUnit`); no new action type.
- **The card does its own work** (self-contained, even if duplicated); the trigger result only
  declares **suppression**.
- **One typed method per trigger** (mirrors `TransactionService`).
- **Abstract `CardTriggerResult` hierarchy** with per-space subtypes (FP gets three suppress
  flags, Tax one) — granularity in the type, not a bare bool.
- **`SuppressDefault` rework:** stop returning it at draw for **kept** cards (the draw-time bug);
  make it **granular + group-scoped**.
- **Advance vs Move:** `Advance` (to a named place) does **not** draw the destination card;
  `MoveSpaces` does; held-triggers fire on both. Needs a "draw the space's card?" flag through
  board resolution.
- **Recursion: loops allowed** (John's instinct) — "all advance to GO" fires GO per player.
  Revisit a guard only if the real list shows a genuine cycle.
- **Blocker:** the finalised card list (the Excel is stale, John provides it in a few days) pins
  the exact trigger set, per-card condition types, and suppress metadata. Architecture is settled
  and buildable now; `AmountSource` is the one model change worth doing up front.
